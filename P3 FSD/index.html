About

Innovation, prototyping and collaboration in one focused day.

MARINOTEX invites students to tackle maritime problems — blue economy, autonomous systems, sustainability, and more. Mentors from industry and academia will help teams validate and refine ideas quickly.


Open to UG students across colleges. AMET students: discounted fee available.

  • Teams up to 4 members
  • Bring laptop, charger, and valid college ID
  • Original work required — plagiarism leads to disqualification
