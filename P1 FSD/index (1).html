What is Pongal?

Pongal is the harvest festival celebrated with gratitude for the year's crops. Our celebration includes traditional Pongal cooking, Kolam (rangoli) competitions, folk dance performances, and games celebrating community spirit.

  • Traditional Pongal cooking demonstration
  • Kolam & Rangoli competitions
  • Music, dance and cultural programs
  • Food stalls & community lunch
